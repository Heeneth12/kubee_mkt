Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Free plan really free forever? +
Yes. No credit card required. The Free plan stays free with no expiry — upgrade only when you need more.
Can I switch plans anytime? +
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Billing is prorated monthly.
Do you support GST billing? +
Yes. All invoices are GST-compliant. You'll receive a proper tax invoice for every payment.
What payment methods do you accept? +
UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit cards, and Razorpay. All major Indian payment methods supported.
